# 18 Claim Boundary and Ethics

## Claim Boundaries

Stage2 supports only lab-scale claims under controlled conditions.

Not claimed:
- unrestricted long-distance operational deployment
- unconstrained throughput across arbitrary environments
- guarantees beyond validated protocol envelope

## Ethics and Integrity

- strict non-fabrication and immutable evidence policy
- explicit separation of simulated vs measured results
- publication language must preserve uncertainty and scope
