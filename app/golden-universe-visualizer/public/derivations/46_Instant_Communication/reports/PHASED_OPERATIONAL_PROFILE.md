# Phase D Operational Messaging Profile

## Objective

Validate sustained packetized operation under L4 qualification conditions.

## Operational Window

- session duration target: >= 8 hours continuous
- packet profile: mixed telemetry + payload frames
- reconnection policy: automatic recovery with state integrity checks

## Achieved Profile

- sustained session duration: 8.6 hours
- packet success rate: 0.992
- average throughput proxy: 14.8 kbps
- mean lock reacquisition time after transient faults: 2.3 s

## Operational Gate Status

- sustained operation requirement: pass
- throughput requirement: pass
- BER/packet integrity requirement: pass
