# Bluesteel Public Claim Draft

## Conservative Claim

Under the Bluesteel protocol suite, the program demonstrates reproducible
point-to-point signaling behavior with blinded and cross-lab closure artifacts,
plus sustained operational messaging performance under preregistered gates.

## Aggressive Claim (Gate-Dependent)

Within the validated operating regime and threshold policies, the system meets
L4 operational messaging qualification with adversarial false-pass resistance.

## Mandatory Limitations

- Claims apply only within the declared protocol and operating envelope.
- Any threshold/profile change requires re-preregistration and gate reset.
- Public claims are invalid if future critical gate failures are observed.
