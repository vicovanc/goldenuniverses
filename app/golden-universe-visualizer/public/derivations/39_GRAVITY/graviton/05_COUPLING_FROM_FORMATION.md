# Graviton Coupling from Formation and Induced Gravity

**The graviton coupling strength is fixed by Newton's constant G_N**

---

## 1. Universal Coupling to Stress-Energy

The graviton couples to the stress-energy tensor $T_{\mu\nu}$ of all matter and fields. The coupling is **universal** (equivalence principle):

$$S_{\text{int}} = -\frac{1}{2} \int d^4x \, h_{\mu\nu} \, T^{\mu\nu}$$

In the full action, the prefactor is determined by the Einstein-Hilbert normalization. The dimensionful coupling constant is:

$$\kappa = \sqrt{8\pi G_N}$$

in natural units where $c = \hbar = 1$. In SI units, the coupling has dimensions of (mass)$^{-1}$ appropriate for a dimension-5 operator $h T$.
